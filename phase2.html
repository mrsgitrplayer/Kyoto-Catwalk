Based on the structure we had established, it was now time to do more concrete and focussed research.
In my case that was researching more on Paul Poiret, the History of the Catwalk and Haute Couture.
During our last meeting we also came across some topics where we were both had not read much about, or where we were not so sure how relevant it would be for our presentation. In these cases we tried to keep them in mind during our research in Phase II, whithout investing too much time in them.
Due to the current situation I did the majority of my research online, using tools such as swissbib, jstor.org, worldcat.org, kubikat.org, academia/researchgate, ProQuest...
